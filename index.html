About Me

Alejandra Lopez, also known as Azur, is a Puerto Rican actor that began her acting career for Theater in the Dominican Republic. Once she moved to Miami she started working in Hispanic television shows such as Cronicas de Sabado and others. She is known for her roles in Modern Family (2019), Jenni Rivera: Mariposa de Barrio (2017) and Mi Familia Perfecta (2018). Alejandra is an actress, makeup artist, and social media personality.

My work varies on creating beautiful creatures to making clients look radiant. Makeup came naturally to me, I was inspired to think outside the box, for years I educated myself, then I moved on to studying at the Cinema Makeup School in Los Angeles. Since then I've been studying and working so I can transform my clients in beauty and special effects. Makeup is a passion and an art form, and any chance of sharing that with a client is an opportunity to inspire those to think outside the box.

Makeup Artistry

I'm passionate about makeup and special effects. Creating unique looks and transforming my clients is what drives me.

Social Media

Career Goals

I aspire to continue growing as an actress and makeup artist, pushing boundaries and inspiring others with my work. Whether it's on-screen or behind the scenes, I want to make a lasting impact in the entertainment industry.
